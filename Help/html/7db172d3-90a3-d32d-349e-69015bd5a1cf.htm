ResetThreshold
Resets the threshold. If lightness of a color is lower than this threshold, the pin will be lowered. A higher threshold leads lighter points to raise pins. A low threshold leads darker pins to stay lowered. Have to be between 0 and 255.
SetThreshold
Sets the threshold. If lightness of a color is lower than this threshold, the pin will be lowered. A higher threshold leads lighter points to raise pins. A low threshold leads darker pins to stay lowered. Have to be between 0 and 255.
